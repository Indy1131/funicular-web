Who are we?

Committed to offering high quality experiences and items.

Funicular is a group based on the ROBLOX online platform that designs User Generated Content for over 100 collaborating groups and creates unique games for users to experience. We practice 3D modeling, texturing, graphic design and programming. In addition to our own offerings, we sell our services for a commission fee.
